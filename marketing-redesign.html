Features

A reading & research copilot, built into the browser.

Designed for long-form web content — articles, documentation, technical references, and business pages.

Reads the page for you

Extracts the readable text of the current tab and uses it as context. No copy-pasting articles, docs, or code.

Side-panel, per tab

Opens in Chrome's side panel with its own session per tab, so switching pages never mixes conversations.

Quick actions

One click to summarize, explain, rewrite, translate, simplify, or explain code — on the page, a selection, or pasted text.

Bring your own key

Use your own OpenAI or OpenRouter key. OpenRouter unlocks Claude, Gemini, and more model families through one key.

Local-first & private

Keys and chat history stay on your machine. Page content is sent only when you ask. Exclude sites or disable history anytime.

Your models, your way

Pick default providers and models, favorite the ones you use, hide the rest. Stop streamed responses mid-answer.

Your keys and history never leave your machine. Page content is sent only when you ask.
